Path to Equity: Key Recommendations

Addressing these multifaceted challenges requires a comprehensive approach to foster genuine inclusivity.

Strengthen Foundational Support

Implement high-quality preparatory/remedial programs. Invest in improving school education in disadvantaged regions.

Enhance Inclusivity & Combat Discrimination

Enforce anti-discrimination policies. Conduct mandatory caste-sensitization training. Ensure effective EOCs/SC-ST Cells. Recruit diverse faculty.

Comprehensive Financial & Welfare Support

Increase financial aid to cover full costs. Establish culturally sensitive mental health services and academic support.

Improve Data Collection & Transparency

Standardize and centralize disaggregated data on admissions, performance, and dropouts. Mandate transparent reporting.
